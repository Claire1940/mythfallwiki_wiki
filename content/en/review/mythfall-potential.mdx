<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Posture Systems">

The true mechanical **mythfall potential** lies in its skill-based action combat. Unlike standard clicker RPGs, this experience utilizes a tactical combat loop built around blocking, parrying, and posture management.

| Combat Action | Mechanical Effect | Risk vs Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Block (Hold F)** | Reduces incoming damage but increases your posture bar. | Safe but leads to a posture break if held too long. |
| **Perfect Parry** | Negates all incoming damage and lowers your current posture. | Requires precise timing; completely staggers the attacker. |
| **Dodge (Press Q)** | Grants temporary invincibility frames to escape danger. | Essential for avoiding unblockable blue strong attacks. |
| **Stagger Loop** | Breaking an enemy's posture bar leaves them completely vulnerable. | Allows you to unleash high-damage weapon and magic skills. |

Combat is further deepened by the inclusion of active skills. Players can hot-swap between physical weapon skill bars (such as the Slash Lunge or Cross Slash) and dedicated magic move sets (like Magic Explosion or Soul Rand) to create devastating offensive chains.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Blue Glow">
Strong attacks cannot be blocked or parried. When an enemy flashes blue, you must use your dodge key (Q) to weave out of the attack radius.
</Callout>
