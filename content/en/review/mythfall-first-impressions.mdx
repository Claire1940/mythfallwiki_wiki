<Section icon={Swords} title="Action Combat & Skill-Based Mechanics">

The standout feature during our **mythfall first impressions** test was undoubtedly the combat system. Unlike traditional Roblox RPGs where spamming left-click is the only viable strategy, combat here demands focus, timing, and defensive awareness. Enemies do not stagger easily from basic attacks, meaning you must actively watch their animations to survive.

The game introduces a robust posture and parry system. Holding the block key increases your posture bar; if it fills completely, your guard breaks, leaving you vulnerable. However, timing your block perfectly triggers a parry, which negates all incoming damage, preserves your posture, and inflicts heavy posture damage on your attacker.

### Combat Systems Comparison

To illustrate how defensive and offensive options balance out, look at how different actions affect your tactical positioning:

| Action | Posture Cost | Damage Reduction | Best Used For |
|--------|--------------|------------------|---------------|
| **Basic Blocking** | High (Increases bar) | 100% Physical | Surviving fast, unpredictable multi-hit combos. |
| **Perfect Parrying** | Low (Reduces bar) | 100% All Damage | Staggering elite enemies and opening counter-windows. |
| **Sprinting/Dodge** | Medium (Stamina) | 100% (I-Frames) | Avoiding massive area-of-effect (AoE) magic attacks. |
| **Heavy Attack** | None | None | Breaking an enemy's block when their posture is high. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Posture">
Spamming block blindly will get you killed quickly in boss encounters. If an elite enemy breaks your posture, you will be stunned for several seconds, which usually results in a swift trip back to the nearest spawn point.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Deep Character Customization: Races & Magic">

Upon launching the game, players enter a detailed character creation screen. Rather than locking you into rigid classes, your character's identity is shaped by your choice of race, weapon masteries, and magic elements. There are five distinct races available at launch, each offering unique passive stat adjustments and aesthetic designs.
