<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat System and Gameplay Mechanics">

The core combat in Mythfall moves away from generic button-mashing and embraces a tactical, action-oriented style. Players must actively manage their positioning, time their parries, and watch their posture meters. 

| Combat Mechanic | Action & Controls | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Parrying** | Right-click to block right before an attack | Negates damage and increases enemy posture buildup. |
| **Dodge** | Press Q during enemy attack frames | Grants brief invincibility frames; essential for unblockable attacks. |
| **Lock-On** | Press Tab to lock onto target | Keeps your camera centered on elite enemies and bosses. |
| **Posture Break** | Deal continuous heavy damage | Staggers elite enemies at 50% and stuns them completely at 100%. |

The game features nine unique weapon types (including Swords, Daggers, Great Axes, and Polearms) and powerful magic spells. This variety allows you to customize your playstyle, whether you prefer fast-paced dual-sword combos or heavy, high-risk axe swings.

<Section icon={Map} title="How Progression Works: Islands and Acts">

Progression in Mythfall is structured around a series of expansive, beautifully crafted islands. Each island features its own storyline split into four distinct acts. Cleared acts unlock new areas, tougher bosses, and advanced crafting mechanics.

| Progression Phase | Content Unlocked | Main Activities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tutorial Act** | Starter island, basic controls | Learn movement, complete first quest, defeat tutorial boss. |
| **Act One** | Character customization, Main World | Select from 5 races, unlock basic magic, start Island One. |
| **Act Two** | Mission boards, multiplayer parties | Group up for elite hunts, unlock blacksmithing and mining. |
| **Act Three** | Dungeons, PvP arenas | Farm endgame gear, participate in structured PvP battles. |

As you move through the acts, you will unlock essential life skills like mining and blacksmithing. These allow you to gather raw materials across the islands and craft up to 16 unique armor sets and weapons, ensuring your build stays competitive.
