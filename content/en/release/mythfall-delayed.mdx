The highly anticipated Roblox MMO RPG **Mythfall delayed** its official launch from its previous targets to a finalized date of **July 10, 2026**. Rather than pushing out a rushed, buggy product, the development team opted for a brief delay to execute essential quality assurance (QA) passes and implement a massive feature request: full mobile support on day one.

- **Platform expansion**: Explains why mobile optimization was prioritized for day one instead of launching exclusively on PC first.
