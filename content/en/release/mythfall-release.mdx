<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Systems: Weapons and Magic Mastery">

Combat in this experience is fast-paced, action-oriented, and heavily reliant on active defense mechanics. You must master blocking, dodging, and parrying to survive high-level PvE encounters and PvP combat.

| Combat Mechanic | Input (PC) | Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Attack** | Left Click (M1) | Fast, low-damage strikes to build combos. |
| **Strong Attack** | R | Heavy shield-breaking or high-damage attacks. |
| **Dodge** | Q | Quick dash to escape area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. |
| **Block / Parry** | Hold F / Tap F | Block reduces incoming damage; a perfectly timed tap parries the attack. |
| **Weapon / Magic Swap** | 1 / 2 | Swap between your active weapon and magic mastery hotbars. |

<Callout type="info" title="Mastery Progression">
Unlike traditional RPGs with rigid class systems, your build is determined by your equipped weapon and magic. Using these combat tools in battle earns you mastery points, which gradually unlock advanced active abilities mapped to your Z, X, and V keys.
</Callout>
