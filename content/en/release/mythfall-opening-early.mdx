The combat in Mythfall is highly reactive, relying heavily on timing and defensive maneuvers. Unlike standard Roblox clicker RPGs, you must actively block, parry, and dodge incoming attacks to survive elite encounters.

| Combat Control | PC Keybind | Mobile Input | Mechanics & Tips |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Attack** | **M1 (Left Click)** | Tap Screen | Basic combo filler; builds meter. |
| **Strong Attack** | **R Key** | Dedicated Button | Heavy damage; can stagger elite enemies. |
| **Dodge / Dash** | **Q Key** | Dash Button | Provides brief invincibility frames. |
| **Block / Parry** | **F Key** | Block Button | Hold to block; tap right before an attack to parry. |
| **Target Lock-on** | **Tab Key** | Lock Icon | Centers camera on the nearest hostile mob. |

<Callout type="success" title="Stagger Mechanic">
When fighting elite enemies, watch their stagger bar. When the bar hits halfway, the elite enemy will briefly be stunned, giving you a perfect window to unleash high-damage weapon skills.
</Callout>
