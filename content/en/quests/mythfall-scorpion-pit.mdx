<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Hitless Strategy">

Achieving a hitless run in the **mythfall scorpion pit** requires strict discipline and a structured plan of attack. Follow this step-by-step progression route to clear the dungeon without taking damage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Entrance Chamber">
    Enter the pit and immediately deploy ranged attacks to eliminate the initial line of crawling scouts before they can fire their first projectile wave.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Spawner Destruction">
    Locate the glowing green nests along the walls. Focus all your primary DPS on these nests immediately; leaving them alive will cause the arena to flood with toxic spitters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Circular Rotation">
    Never stand still. Move in a continuous, wide circle around the perimeter of the central chamber. This coaxes enemy projectile spreads to target your trailing location rather than your path ahead.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bait and Punish the Boss">
    During the final boss encounter, wait for the heavy tail-slam attack to finish. Once the boss is briefly locked in its recovery animation, unleash your highest damage abilities before retreating.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Condition">
By maintaining this rotation, you ensure that the spawners are cleared before they can accumulate, keeping the screen clean and manageable.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Enemy Types and Attack Patterns">

Understanding your enemies is half the battle. The pit features several distinct creature types, each with its own projectile behavior and movement speed. 

<Table>
| Enemy Type | Primary Attack Pattern | How to Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Acid Spitter** | Linear toxic projectile shots | Side-step immediately; do not back up in a straight line |
| **Cavern Lurker** | Fast melee lunges | Use knockback spells or dodge backward to reset range |
| **Broodmother (Boss)** | 360-degree radial bullet rings | Stand at mid-range and slip through the widening gaps |
| **Larval Swarm** | Tracking homing charges | Use quick area-of-effect spells to clear them instantly |

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Note">
The radial bullet rings fired by the Broodmother expand as they travel. Standing too close makes dodging almost impossible, while standing too far limits your damage output. Find the sweet spot at mid-range.
