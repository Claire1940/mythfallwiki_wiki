The main progression loop in Mythfall begins with a structured tutorial sequence that teaches you essential movement and combat mechanics. Before you can freely explore the expansive islands of Act 1 and Act 2, you must complete the linear story introduction. 

**Video Highlights:**

Your first quest objective is to travel across the vines toward the **Divine Tree**. This sequence acts as a safe environment to get used to the action-RPG controls before you face the open world.

<Callout type="tip" title="Defensive Tip">
Do not ignore the blocking prompt during the tutorial. Holding the block key increases your posture bar, but perfectly parrying attacks negates all incoming damage and prevents your posture from breaking.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Act 1 Story Quest Progression">

Once you arrive at the Divine Tree and defeat the initial hostile celestial, you will transition into the Act 1 story quests. Act 1 introduces character customization, five distinct races (Human, Druid, Minotaur, Duskorn, and Nephilim), and the core questing loop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starting Race">
    Choose your character's race during the opening cutscene. Each race offers unique passive stat modifiers, such as critical strength or ether capacity, which will influence your combat style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defend the Village">
    Follow the prompt to assist the villagers under attack. This quest introduces basic group combat, where you will fight alongside friendly NPCs to clear waves of low-level mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Your First Active Ability">
    Defeat enemies to gain combat mastery. Once your mastery bar levels up, equip your first active ability (such as Cross Slash or Magic Slash) via your moveset hotkey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confront the Syndicate Leader">
    Locate and fight the Syndicate Leader boss at the end of the Act 1 zone. Focus on parrying his heavy physical attacks to stagger his posture, leaving him open to high-damage magic attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Warning">
Spamming dodge or special moves will quickly deplete your stamina and ether resources, leaving you vulnerable to the Syndicate Leader's grab attacks. Always keep a small reserve of stamina to perform a quick block.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Act 2 Island Exploration and Quest Hubs">

Completing Act 1 unlocks full access to the massive Island One map. Act 2 expands the game mechanics significantly by introducing multiplayer partying, mission boards, blacksmithing, mining, and advanced gear vendors.

### Quest Types and Mission Boards
Instead of following a single linear path, Act 2 allows players to pick up repeatable side quests and bounty missions from the local village mission boards.

| Quest Type | Main Objective | Primary Rewards | Best Build Strategy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Story Quests** | Escort NPCs, retrieve artifacts, clear dungeons | High EXP, story progression, unique armor | Balanced weapon and magic setups |
| **Bounty Missions** | Hunt specific elite monsters or minibosses | Gold, rare crafting materials, weapon mastery | Solo-friendly high-damage combat builds |
| **Gathering Tasks** | Mine ore veins, collect herbs, trade items | Blacksmithing materials, vendor reputation | High-mobility classes with fast movement |
