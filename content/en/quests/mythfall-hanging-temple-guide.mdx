<Section icon={Swords} title="Enemy Breakdown and Combat Mechanics">

The Hanging Temple is heavily guarded by specialized monk enemies. Unlike the slow-moving monsters found in early-game zones, these monks are incredibly agile, capable of closing distances rapidly and launching dense bullet-hell patterns. Understanding how to counter each enemy type is essential for keeping your character alive.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Temple Monk">
    - **Behavior**: Patrols in groups of 2 to 3.
    - **Attacks**: Rapid-fire light magic projectiles.
    - **Strategy**: Keep your distance and use obstacles to block their line of sight.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Elite Monk">
    - **Behavior**: Guards narrow bridges and gateways.
    - **Attacks**: Heavy ground pounds and knockback waves.
    - **Strategy**: Dodge behind them when they wind up their attacks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Summoner Monk">
    - **Behavior**: Stays in the backline of combat rooms.
    - **Attacks**: Summons minor spirits to distract players.
    - **Strategy**: Focus fire on them immediately to prevent getting overwhelmed.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

To deal with these threats efficiently, players should select weapon builds that emphasize either extreme range or high-area-of-effect (AoE) damage. The following combat comparison table highlights the effectiveness of different weapon setups:

| Weapon Type | Range Rating | Crowd Control | Solo Performance | Strategy Notes |

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Zone">
Many combat rooms feature open edges. Monk attacks carry significant knockback. Getting hit near an edge will knock you off the platform, resulting in instant death and loss of accumulated experience. Always fight with your back to a solid wall.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Dungeon Walkthrough">

Clearing the Hanging Temple requires a methodical approach. Rushing through the corridors will trigger multiple enemy spawns, leaving you trapped with nowhere to dodge. Follow this step-by-step progression route to complete the dungeon safely:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Entrance Courtyard">
    Eliminate the initial patrol of Temple Monks guarding the outer gate. Use the pillars for cover to avoid their opening bullet salvos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Cliffside Bridges">
    Cross the narrow wooden walkways carefully. Watch out for spawning Elite Monks that attempt to block your path. Use slow, methodical movements to ensure you do not slip off the edge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Inner Chambers">
    Enter the main temple halls. Here, you will face mixed waves of Temple Monks and Summoner Monks. Prioritize the summoners in the back before dealing with the agile melee units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for the Boss Arena">
    Before stepping into the final chamber, replenish your health and mana pools. Ensure your best offensive and defensive skills are off cooldown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Checkpoint Tip">
Once you clear the Inner Chambers, the path back to the entrance is relatively safe. If your inventory is full of mid-tier loot, consider returning to the bank in town to secure your items before initiating the final boss fight.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Defeat the Floating Boss Monk">

The final encounter in the Hanging Temple is a battle against the floating boss monk. This boss does not walk on the ground; instead, he floats dynamically around the arena, launching complex, high-speed bullet-hell patterns that cover large portions of the room.

To defeat him, you must learn his phases and exploit his brief moments of vulnerability:

| Boss Phase | Attack Patterns | Player Movement Strategy | Vulnerability Window |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1 (100% - 70% HP)** | Direct magic projectiles and light teleports. | Circle-strafe around the outer edge of the arena. | Constant; boss moves slowly between attacks. |
| **Phase 2 (70% - 30% HP)** | Radial bullet waves and rapid teleportation. | Stay mid-range; dodge between the gaps of the waves. | Briefly pauses immediately after a teleport. |
| **Phase 3 (Under 30% HP)** | Arena-wide storm, summoning helper spirits. | Focus on survival; destroy helper spirits first. | High damage window when helper spirits are cleared. |

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Strategy">
Do not chase the boss when he teleports. Instead, position yourself in the center of the arena and wait for him to reappear. Using a high-DPS ranged weapon or magic build allows you to deal consistent damage while focusing almost entirely on dodging his projectile patterns.
</Callout>
