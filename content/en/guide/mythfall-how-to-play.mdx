The control scheme is designed to give you complete control over your character's combat flow. Below is the default control configuration for PC players:

| Keybind | Action | Combat Function / Usage |
| [LMB] | Primary Attack | Execute basic melee or ranged weapon strikes |
| [RMB] | Block / Heavy Attack | Absorb incoming damage or break enemy guards |
| [Q] | Weapon Skill | Trigger your equipped weapon's unique ability |
| [E] | Interact | Talk to NPCs, open chests, or gather resources |
| [R] | Special Ability | Cast powerful magic or race-specific skills |
| [SHIFT] | Sprint / Dodge | Evade enemy attacks with invincibility frames |
| [CTRL] | Slide / Crouch | Navigate tight spaces or initiate quick slides |
| [SPACE] | Jump / Wall Launch | Perform basic jumps or launch off vertical walls |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
All keybinds are fully customizable. If you are playing on Mobile or Console, head to the in-game settings menu to adjust touch-button placement or controller layouts for better response times.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="First Session Route: Step-by-Step Tutorial">

When you first launch the game, your main goal is to establish a strong progression loop. Navigating the initial phase efficiently will save you hours of backtracking.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Initial Tutorial">
    Enter the starting zone and follow the tutorial sequence. This phase introduces the core movement, basic combat, and quest structures. Do not skip this, as completing it is required to unlock the wider world.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize the AFK World">
    If the servers are undergoing maintenance or you need a break, spend time in the AFK World. This system allows you to passively stack up Astro shards and character rerolls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll Your Race and Customization">
    Use your earned rerolls in the main menu to customize your character. Mythfall features five unique races with distinct traits. Aim for a race that complements your intended playstyle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Embark on Island One: Act One">
    Once prepared, sail to Island One. Progression on each island is split into four distinct Acts. Focus entirely on the Act One questline to unlock local vendors and basic blacksmithing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Warning">
Do not wander into high-level island zones too early. Each Act scales in difficulty, and enemies in later acts will easily defeat under-geared players. Stick to your active quest targets.
</Callout>

</Section>

To help you choose, review this comparison of the primary combat paths:

| Combat Path | Primary Focus | Skill Ceiling | Best Situations |
| Melee Weapons | Close-quarters combat, blocking, and parrying | Medium | Solo boss encounters, tight spaces |
| Ranged Weapons | Keeping distance, consistent DPS, kiting | High | Party-based dungeon runs, open fields |
