Use the following control checklist to verify your keybinds before engaging tough enemies:

| Input Action | PC Keybind | Mobile Layout | Combat Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Light Attack** | Left Click (LMB) | Tap Weapon Icon | Quick physical combos and basic energy regeneration |
| **Strong Attack** | Heavy Key / RMB | Hold Weapon Icon | Shield-breaking strikes that leave enemies vulnerable |

| **Skill 1 / 2** | Number Keys (1-4) | Skill Hotbar | Casts mastered weapon movesets or powerful magics |

Before setting foot in the main world, you will customize your character. The choices you make here regarding race and weapon synergy will define your early-game efficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Roll Your Race">
    Choose from one of the five unique races during character creation. Each race provides passive stat modifiers, such as increased critical strength, faster ether regeneration, or health scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Starting Weapon">
    Pick one of the nine available weapon types. Light weapons like daggers offer rapid attacks and high mobility, while heavy weapons like greatswords deal massive posture damage at the cost of speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Your First Magic">
    Equip a magic style that complements your weapon choice. For example, if you chose a slow melee weapon, equip utility or crowd-control magic to keep enemies at bay.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Level Up Mastery">
    Engage in combat to earn mastery points. Do not split your focus; keep using the same weapon and magic type until you unlock their respective tier-two active skills.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Racial Synergy">

</Callout>
