<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Controls and Movement">

In any **mythfall tutorial**, the most important lesson is that combat is active, not static. You cannot simply stand still and trade blows with enemies. The game utilizes a "hit-box" system where dodging and blocking are mandatory for survival, especially during boss encounters.

| Action | PC Keybind | Mobile/Console Input |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | Left Click | Tap Screen / R2 |
| **Block** | F | Shield Icon / L2 |
| **Dodge / Dash** | Left Shift | Dash Button / Circle |
| **Magic Skills** | 1, 2, 3, 4 | Skill Hotbar |
| **Interaction** | E | Proximity Prompt |

Mastering the "Dodge-Cancel" is a pro-level tactic. By dashing immediately after a heavy weapon swing, you can negate the recovery animation, allowing you to reposition before an enemy counters. This is particularly useful when using slower, high-damage weapons like Greatswords.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Movement Advice">
Practice your climbing and stamina management in the starter village. Many hidden chests and NPC quest-givers are located on high cliffs that require efficient pathfinding to reach without exhausting your stamina bar.
</Callout>

</Section>

The core of the Mythfall experience lies in its **9 weapon types** and diverse magic schools. As you use a specific weapon, you gain mastery, unlocking unique skills that can turn the tide of battle.

**Weapon Categories:**
1. **Shortswords**: Fast attacks, low range.
2. **Greatswords**: High damage, slow recovery.
3. **Spears**: Excellent reach, piercing damage.
4. **Bows**: Long-range, requires ammo management.
5. **Staves**: Primary tool for pure magic builds.
6. **Daggers**: High critical hit chance, stealth-focused.
7. **Hammers**: Blunt damage, effective against armored foes.
8. **Axes**: Balanced speed and power.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Optimal Leveling and Progression Route">

To progress efficiently in the **mythfall tutorial** path, you must follow a structured route. Wandering aimlessly can lead to high-level zones where enemies will easily overpower you.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Awakening Questline">
    This initial quest chain introduces you to the basic NPCs, provides your first weapon, and explains the magic system. Do not skip the dialogue, as it marks your map with essential locations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Second Island">
    Once you reach Level 10, the ferryman will allow you to travel to the next island. This area contains higher-tier ores like Copper and Tin, necessary for your first gear upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Join a Party for Dungeons">
    Around Level 20, solo progression slows down. Join a party to tackle the "Whispering Caves" dungeon. The XP gains here are significantly higher than overworld grinding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Focus on Mastery Grinding">
    Pick one main weapon and one magic element. Spreading your points too thin early on will result in a weak character. Maximize one style to unlock its "Ultimate" ability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Participate in World Events">
    Keep an eye on the server announcements. World bosses spawn every 4 hours and provide the best materials for blacksmithing legendary-tier equipment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Efficiency">
The best farming spots are usually near "Mana Wells." These locations replenish your mana faster, allowing you to use your magic skills more frequently while grinding mobs for XP.
</Callout>

</Section>
