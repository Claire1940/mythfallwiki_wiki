<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Character Creation Guide">

For new players entering the Roblox experience, following a structured approach to your first build is highly recommended. Use these steps to ensure your **mythfall character creation** results in a viable hero for the 2026 meta.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Race Selection">
    Open the creation menu and select your race. Look closely at the passive bonuses. If you plan on being a frontline tank, choose a race with high base HP. If you prefer magic, look for Mana regeneration buffs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Physical Customization">
    Mythfall offers deep cosmetic options. While these don't affect stats, your character's height can slightly impact your hitboxes in PvP. Most players prefer a standard height for balanced play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Selecting Your First Weapon">
    The tutorial will offer you a choice of basic weapons. Test the "feel" of the animations. Attack speed and recovery frames are crucial in Mythfall's action-combat system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assigning Starting Attributes">
    If the current build allows for initial point allocation, put 60% into your primary damage stat (Strength/Dexterity/Intelligence) and 40% into Vitality for survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Entering the World">
    Finalize your character and enter the starter island. Immediately look for the first Story Quest NPC to begin your leveling journey and unlock your first Magic ability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Ready for Adventure">
Once you have completed these steps, your character is ready. Remember that while your race is permanent, your weapon and magic choices can be pivoted later in the game.
</Callout>
