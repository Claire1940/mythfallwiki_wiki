<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Encounters and Dungeon Mechanics">

The **mythfall gameplay reveal** showcased several high-level PvE encounters, including massive world bosses and instanced dungeons. These fights are designed around "mechanics" rather than just "damage sponging." ### Boss Strategy Fundamentals

1.  **Observation Phase**: Most bosses have 2-3 distinct phases. Use the first minute of the fight to learn their attack patterns without over-committing your stamina.
2.  **Stamina Management**: Dodging and blocking consume stamina. If you run out, you will be unable to avoid heavy hits.
3.  **Party Synergy**: In dungeons, ensure your party has a mix of damage dealers and support magic users. Coordination is key for interrupting boss ultimate abilities.

| Boss Type | Common Mechanic | Recommended Build |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Beast-Type** | Fast lunges and area-of-effect (AoE) slams | High Agility / Twin Blades |
| **Arcane-Type** | Ranged projectile spam and teleportation | High Magic Resistance / Bow |
| **Titan-Type** | Slow, massive damage hits and shield phases | Greatsword / Heavy Armor |
| **Swarm-Type** | Summons multiple smaller minions | AoE Magic / Spear |

<Callout type="warning" title="Solo vs Party">
