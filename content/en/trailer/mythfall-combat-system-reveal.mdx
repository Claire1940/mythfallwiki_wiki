<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Mechanics: Blocking and Dodging">

The **mythfall combat system reveal** emphasized that a good offense is nothing without a solid defense. Mythfall features a robust defensive system that includes blocking, dodging, and parrying. Unlike older RPGs where you might just stand still and tank hits, Mythfall requires you to actively read enemy "telegraphs"—visual or auditory cues that signal an incoming attack.

**Key Defensive Actions:**
- **Perfect Block (Parry)**: Blocking at the exact moment of impact negates all damage and staggers the attacker.
- **Dodge Roll**: Provides "invincibility frames" (i-frames) that allow you to pass through attacks safely.
- **Stamina Management**: Every defensive action consumes stamina. If your stamina hits zero, your guard will break, leaving you stunned.
