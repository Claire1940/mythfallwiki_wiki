<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Controls & Combat Setup">

MythFall features an action-heavy combat system that heavily penalizes button-mashing. To succeed, players must learn the exact timing of dodges, blocks, and parries. Below is the official control scheme optimized for PC players:

| Action | PC Keyboard Input | Combat Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | W, A, S, D | Standard character directional movement |
| **Sprint** | Hold Shift | Rapid movement, consumes stamina over time |
| **Equip Sword** | E | Draws or sheathes your primary physical weapon |
| **Dodge** | Q | Quick dash with temporary invincibility frames |
| **Climb** | G | Scales vertical walls and environmental obstacles |
| **Block** | Hold F | Mitigates incoming damage at the cost of posture |
| **Parry** | Press F (Timed) | Deflects attacks perfectly, staggering the enemy |
| **Light Attack** | Left Click (M1) | Fast, low-damage combos to build energy |
| **Strong Attack** | R | Heavy, slow strike that deals massive posture damage |
| **Weapon Moveset** | 1 | Accesses your primary weapon's active abilities |
| **Magic Moveset** | 2 | Switches to your equipped magical spells |
| **Abilities** | Z, X, V | Triggers slot-specific combat maneuvers |

For mobile players, these inputs are mapped to a clean, dynamic touch interface that scales automatically based on your screen size.

<Callout type="success" title="Master the Parry Timing">
Parrying is the most powerful defensive tool in MythFall. Pressing **F** right before an enemy strike lands will completely negate damage and trigger a stagger state, allowing you to follow up with a high-damage Strong Attack (R).
</Callout>
