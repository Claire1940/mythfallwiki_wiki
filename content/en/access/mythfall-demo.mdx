<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Encounters and Party Dynamics">

The **Mythfall demo** features challenging PvE boss fights that require more than just raw damage. Bosses have specific mechanics, telegraphs, and phases. While solo play is possible for highly skilled players, the game is designed around multiplayer parties. Teaming up with friends allows for role distribution, such as having one player focus on drawing aggro while others deal damage or provide support.

**Essential Boss Prep Checklist:**
- Upgrade your primary weapon to at least **Level 5** via blacksmithing.
- Ensure your **stamina potions** are stocked for frequent dodging.
- Coordinate magic abilities with your party to maximize **status effects**.

| Boss Type | Common Mechanic | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Island Guardians** | Large AOE ground slams | Jump or dash outward during the wind-up |
| **Elite Humanoids** | Fast parry-and-riposte | Wait for their combo to end before attacking |
| **Dungeon Bosses** | Multi-phase transitions | Save your high-damage magic for the final phase |

<Callout type="warning" title="Party Efficiency">
In a party of 12, communication is key. Avoid stacking all players in one spot, as many bosses in Mythfall use wide-sweeping attacks that can wipe an entire group instantly.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Crafting, Trading, and Economy">

Beyond combat, the **Mythfall demo** introduces a robust economic system. Players are encouraged to engage in mining, blacksmithing, and trading to acquire the best gear. Blacksmithing allows you to refine raw ores into powerful weapon components, which is often more effective than relying solely on random drops from enemies.

**Economic Activities:**
- **Mining**: Found across all islands; higher-tier ores are located in dangerous zones.
- **Blacksmithing**: Use anvils in major hubs to upgrade weapon stats and durability.
- **Trading**: A player-to-player system allows for the exchange of rare materials and weapons.
