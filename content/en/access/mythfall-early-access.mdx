<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mythfall Early Access Progression: First Session Route">

Starting your journey in **mythfall early access** can be overwhelming due to the sheer size of the open world. Following a structured route during your first hour will ensure you don't waste resources or time on low-yield activities.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Spawn and Controls">
    Enter the Roblox experience and spend the first 5 minutes testing your movement. Practice dodging and blocking immediately, as these are the core mechanics for surviving even basic island mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Story Quest Loop">
    Locate the primary quest giver at the starting hub. Story-driven quests are the most efficient way to gain experience and unlock access to neighboring islands. Do not wander aimlessly until you have a basic weapon set.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Weapon and Magic Testing">
    Try out different weapon drops early. Since there are 9 types, you should find one that matches your reaction speed. Simultaneously, equip your first magic ability to see how it combos with your melee strikes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Gathering">
    As you travel between quest markers, keep an eye out for mining nodes. Mining and blacksmithing are vital for upgrading your gear. Trading with other players in the hub can also help you secure rare materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Tip">
Focus on mastering one weapon type to high proficiency before switching. Mastery progression significantly increases your damage output and unlocks advanced skills that are necessary for mid-game dungeons.
</Callout>

By the end of your first session, you should aim to be at least level 10 with a full set of basic gear and a clear understanding of the map's layout.
